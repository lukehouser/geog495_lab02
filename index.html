Bicycle Sharing Systems
Bicycle sharing systems are a new phenomenon that allow users to use electic assisted bikes for a low fare. Numerous companies, such as Lime or Uber Bicycles, have made headways into the industry and the results are clear to see in any major metropolitan area. These systems have seen a rise in popularity nearly entirely within the last decade. As a resident of Seattle, I have seen the ascent of this mode of transportation first hand, and have become very interested in how these systems work. I have found this new method of transporation to be very helpful to me personally, as they are capable of turning what would be an hour walk into a 15 minute bike ride, saving me huge amounts of time in a pinch.
